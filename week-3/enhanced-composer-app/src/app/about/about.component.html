Johann Pachelbel



Stefany Andrade

Johann Pachelbel was a German composer and teacher often associated with bringing the south German organ schools to their peak. During his lifetime his music was largely successful amassing him many followers and pupils alike. His most well known song is Canon in D, otherwise known as Pachelbel's Canon. He loved to experiment with different instrumentation, sounds, and techniques.
